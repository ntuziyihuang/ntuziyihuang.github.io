The difference between Strategic Planning and Strategic thinking.

The difference between Strategic Planning and Strategic thinking Strategic planning is the process by which leaders define an organization’s direction and allocate resources to move it forward. It sets the framework — the goals, timelines, and initiatives — that guide collective action. Strategic thinking, on the other hand, is how individuals and teams translate those high-level goals into practical paths under real-world constraints. It involves understanding the organization’s long-term objectives and making creative, forward-looking decisions that account for the industry environment, customer needs, and competitive landscape. ...
